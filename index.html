Travel to Houten

Houten is a place of residence and municipality in the Dutch province of Utrecht. It is located southeast of the city of Utrecht between the A12, A27 and the Lek rivers. The municipality has 49,902 inhabitants (January 1, 2019, source: CBS) and has a total area of approximately 59 square kilometers.
